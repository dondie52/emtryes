What we do

Services for service trucks — from the tyre up.

Four service lines built around OTR tyres and the wheels they sit on. Brief us with your tyre or rim spec and we’ll come back with scope, turnaround and a fair quote.

01 — Repairs

OTR tyre repairs

We assess, scope and repair off-the-road tyres — from spot repairs through to major section work. Every repair is documented and pressure-tested before it leaves the floor.

  • Spot & nail-hole repairsWorkshop
  • Section repairs (sidewall & tread)Workshop
  • Bead & shoulder rebuildWorkshop
  • Pre-mount inspection & reportWorkshop

02 — Wheels & Rims

OTR wheels & rims

Multi-piece wheel and rim work for haul trucks, loaders and graders — supply, refurbishment and component replacement, with NDT inspection where it’s required.

Supply

New and reconditioned multi-piece OTR wheels and rims, sourced for fit and life.

Refurbishment

Strip, clean, inspect, re-coat. Bring tired rims back into rotation.

Components

Lock rings, side rings, O-rings and flange replacement to OEM spec.

03 — Accessories

Tyre accessories & consumables

The hardware your fitters reach for first. We stock the right parts so a tyre change doesn’t stall on a missing valve extension.

Valves & extensions

Heavy-duty valves, locking nuts and extensions for OTR and commercial wheel positions.

Mounting paste & lube

Bead lubricants and mounting compounds — the consumables that protect the casing.

Pressure equipment

Gauges, inflators and chuck assemblies rated for the pressures OTR tyres actually run at.

Workshop hardware

The small parts that keep change-outs running — clamps, lock-rings, hand tools.
