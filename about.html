About

One company. All your service truck needs.

EM Tyre Specialists is a Jwaneng-based OTR tyre and wheel business. We work with the trucks that keep mining and civil operations moving — and we treat every repair like the next shift depends on it.

Who we are

A workshop, not a warehouse.

Our focus is narrow on purpose: off-the-road tyre repairs, OTR wheels and rims, accessories and the safety hardware that goes with them. We’re the team you call when a truck’s tyre is the difference between a working shift and an idle pit.

We don’t pretend to be a passenger workshop, a fleet manager or a brand reseller. We do one thing well, and we do it for the people who already know what they need.

What we work on

Service-truck OTR.

Haul trucks, loaders, graders, dozers, water carts — the support fleet that makes a mine site or civil project actually run. Tyres, wheels, rims and the accessories around them.

  • OTR tyre repairs & section work
  • Multi-piece wheel & rim service
  • Tyre accessories & consumables
  • OTR tyre supply & LVD wheel chocks
How we work

Four habits we don’t cut.

No process for its own sake. Just the discipline that keeps repairs honest and change-outs safe.

Inspect first, scope second

Every tyre is assessed before it’s priced. If it’s not safe to repair, we’ll say so.

Document the work

Repairs are recorded so you know exactly what was done and when it was returned to service.

Pressure-test before sign-off

Nothing leaves the floor without a pressure test — the cheapest insurance there is.

Chock first, change second

LVD wheel chocks go in before tools come out. Standard practice, every job.
