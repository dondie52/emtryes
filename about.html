OUR STORY.

We specialize in supplying and fitting tyres for light duty vehicles (LDV) and heavy duty vehicles (HDV), with particular expertise in mining and heavy hauling operations. Our comprehensive range includes OTR (Off-The-Road) tyres for mining equipment and extreme terrain applications.

Our commitment to tyre safety and maintenance ensures our clients operate with confidence and reliability across Botswana's most demanding environments — from the urban streets of Gaborone to the deep pits of the Jwaneng diamond mine.

With certified technicians, premium brand partnerships, and two strategically located service centers, we've built a reputation as the trusted partner for fleets and operators who cannot afford downtime.
