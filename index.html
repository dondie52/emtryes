Our services

Built for the trucks that keep operations moving.

Four core service lines, each focused on the machinery that mining, civil and transport fleets depend on every day.

OTR Tyre Repairs

Section repairs, spot repairs and inspections for off-the-road tyres. We rebuild the tyres you can’t afford to write off.

  • Casing & sidewall repair
  • Bead & shoulder rebuild
  • Pre-mount inspection

OTR Wheels & Rims

Supply, refurbishment and certification of multi-piece OTR wheels and rims for haul trucks, loaders and graders.

  • New & reconditioned rims
  • NDT crack testing
  • Component replacement

Tyre Accessories

Valves, extensions, gauges, lubricants and the consumables your workshop and on-site team actually need to keep going.

  • Valve hardware
  • Mounting paste & lube
  • Pressure equipment

OTR Tyres & LVD Wheel Chocks

OTR tyre supply paired with LVD wheel chocks — the safety pairing every change-out and servicing operation should default to.

  • OTR tyre supply
  • LVD wheel chocks
  • Site safety consultation
